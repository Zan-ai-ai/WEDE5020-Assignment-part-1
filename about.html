Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bright Africa began as a small mentorship programme in Soweto, South Africa. Through community outreach and school partnerships, the initiative rapidly grew. By 2020, Bright Africa had expanded into several African countries, running scholarship programmes and digital bootcamps in local communities.

Thousands of youths have gained the skills and resources to access higher education and build careers in technology.

Mission

To empower underprivileged youth across Africa with scholarships, technology training and mentorship that break poverty cycles and build sustainable futures.

Vision

An Africa where every young person—regardless of background—has the knowledge, skills and opportunities to thrive in the digital age.
